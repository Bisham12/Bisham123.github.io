Living in Korea

Korea is a vibrant country with a rich cultural heritage and modern innovations. Here are some highlights of life in Korea:

  • Food: Korean cuisine, including dishes like kimchi, bulgogi, and bibimbap, is world-famous.
  • Culture: Traditional music, dance, and Hanbok (traditional attire) reflect Korea's history.
  • Landscapes: Korea offers stunning mountains, beaches, and cities like Seoul and Busan.
